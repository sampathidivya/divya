Gangs of godavari


Description:

Gangs of Godavari is a 2024 Indian Telugu-language action drama film written and directed by Krishna Chaitanya.[1] It is being produced by Suryadevara Naga Vamsi and Sai Soujanya; co-produced by Venkat Upputuri and Gopichand Innamuri, under Sithara Entertainments and Fortune Four Cinemas.[2] It stars Vishwak Sen, Anjali, Neha Shetty, Nassar, P. Sai Kumar and Hyper Aadi.
